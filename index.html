Hi, I'm Philipp

I am a Neuroscientist, Psychologist and Engineer. ​In my work in the Clinical Neurotechnology Lab at the Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin, I develop adaptive non-invasive brain stimulation technology to investigate mechanisms of human cognition and treat neuropsychiatric disorders.
