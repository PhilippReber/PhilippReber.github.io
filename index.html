Welcome

I use this site to share project updates and code

I am a Neuroscientist with a background in Psychology and Engineering. I have 3+ years of experience in neuroscientific and psychological research, including 1.5+ years of in-depth applied experience working with human electrophysiology (EEG), non-invasive brain stimulation (tACS, TMS), signal analysis (Python, Matlab), machine learning and advanced statistics.

In my work, I develop non-invasive brain stimulation technology to revolutionize how we interact with the human brain. ​My research questions include how to build back from cortical dysrhythmia and aberrant connectivity in psychiatric illness using personalized closed-loop neuromodulation.

This is a work in progress.
