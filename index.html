Philipp Reber

I'm a Neuroscientist an Engineer a Psychologist|
In my work with Magnetic Tides Inc. at UC Berkeley I build novel adaptive brain stimulation systems to decode and support cognition.
Projects
Pushing the limits of brain machine interaction with novel adaptive brain stimulation systems. Through my work, I aim to develop a better understanding of how cognitive function emerges from the complex interaction of brain networks. I study the electrophysiological patterns of the brain to identify novel targets for non-invasive brain stimulation. I build novel adaptive brain stimulation systems that target the brain with increased precision, flexibility and strength. I strive for developing more effective neurotechnological treatments for those suffering from mental health conditions.
