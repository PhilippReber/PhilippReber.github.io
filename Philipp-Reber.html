Home  / About

I am a Neuroscientist, Psychologist and Engineer. In my work in the Clinical Neurotechnology Lab at the Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin, I use non-invasive brain stimulation technology to understand and manipulate neural mechanisms in the human brain. My research questions include how to build back from rhythmic disturbances and dysfunctional connectivity in psychiatric illness.
