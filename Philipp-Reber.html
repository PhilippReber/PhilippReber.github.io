I am a Neuroscientist, Psychologist and Engineer. In my work in the Clinical Neurotechnology Lab at the Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin, I use non-invasive brain stimulation technology to understand neural mechanisms in the brain and to manipulate cognition and behavior.

My research goals include developing closed-loop brain stimulation systems that adapt to ongoing brain activity and modulate neurons according to the requirements of the target in each moment. My main interests are to investigate rhythmic mechanisms of the healthy brain and to work towards correcting rhythmic disturbances present in psychiatric disorders.
